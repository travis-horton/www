I'm Travis. I studied piano for 6 years at UNC-School of the Arts and 2 more years at the New England Conservatory. I moved to New York City in 2011 and worked as a pianist there for 7 years—played Carnegie Hall, Joe's Pub, off-Broadway shows, worked for NYU and the Brooklyn Youth Chorus as a vocal coach and staff pianist. I rode my bicycle a lot.


Last year, my wife and I decided to leave NYC for the road, and we bought an RV and did just that. My plan was to study programming part time during that year while I worked half-time as a bookkeeper for the Brooklyn Youth Chorus. Now I've done a year of part time programming and here's some of my work from that, along with a sort of archive of my website as a pianist.


I applied and got into the Recurse Center! I'll be in New York City again from early February through May 9th.
